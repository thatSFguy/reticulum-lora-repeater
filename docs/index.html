Trouble? Manual flashing

If the board didn't reboot on its own, double-tap its reset button (within ~500 ms) to force the bootloader, then click Select bootloader port & flash and pick the new port. Or download the UF2 and drag it onto the board's bootloader USB drive (XIAOBOOT / NICENANO / FTHR840BOOT).

Battery calibration

Measure the actual battery (or USB rail) voltage with a multimeter, enter it below, click Calibrate. The firmware computes a fresh multiplier from the raw ADC reading and stages it. Commit to persist.
