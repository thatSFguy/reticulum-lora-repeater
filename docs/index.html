What this does

Flash the Reticulum LoRa Repeater firmware onto a fresh board straight from this page, then connect over USB to read its live status, edit the persistent Config, and calibrate the battery ADC from a multimeter reading. Nothing touches flash until you click a Commit or Flash button.

Flash firmware

Pick a version below, click Download, then click Enter DFU mode to put the running board into bootloader mode automatically. Finally click Flash and select the new bootloader port that appears in the picker. If Enter DFU mode doesn't work (rare), fall back to double-tapping the reset button on the board.

Or upload a local firmware.zip (for developers building their own)

Battery calibration

Measure the actual battery (or USB rail) voltage with a multimeter, enter it in millivolts, click Calibrate. The firmware will compute a fresh multiplier from the raw ADC reading and stage it. Commit to persist.
